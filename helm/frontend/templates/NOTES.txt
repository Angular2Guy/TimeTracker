TimeTracker frontend has been deployed as a Knative Service.

Get the service URL:
  kubectl get ksvc {{ include "frontend.fullname" . }}

Watch the service scale from zero:
  kubectl get pods -l serving.knative.dev/service={{ include "frontend.fullname" . }} -w

Check revision status:
  kubectl get revision -l serving.knative.dev/service={{ include "frontend.fullname" . }}

To curl the service (if using Kourier with minikube):
  URL=$(kubectl get ksvc {{ include "frontend.fullname" . }} -o jsonpath='{.status.url}')
  echo $URL
